Heavy-duty transportation is notoriously hard to decarbonize due to its reliance on energy-dense and cheap liquid fuels.
We are here to change that.

01
The Fuel

Liquid Organic Hydrogen Carriers

LOHCs chemically store hydrogen power in a stable liquid oil. It's non-flammable, easy to handle, and compatible with existing diesel infrastructure.

Pipeline Transportation

LOHCs can tap into the country's extensive fuel distribution network. This includes more than 60,000 mi of fuel pipelines and 100,000 gas stations today.

02
The Key

Our breakthrough technology

Our Hydrogen Release Unit (HRU) is the key to using LOHC as a fuel. Our MIT-patented technology efficiently extracts hydrogen from LOHC safely and efficiently.

Hydrogen Release Unit (HRU)

LOHC enters our HRU, where hydrogen is extracted on demand. The hydrogen can then power an engine or fuel cell, while the used liquid is sent back to the tank.

03
Net Zero

Decarbonize > 1 GT CO2

We are tackling all industries that have been reliant on cheap petrochemical fuels and are notoriosly hard to decarbonize. Whether in trucks, ships, or trains, our technology enables a practical and cost-effective path to zero emissions.

Transportation

We are a true drop-in solution for heavy-duty transportation. We provide the same power, range, and cost to be competitive with diesel today.

Meet The Team

We’re a team of MIT engineers who built our first prototype in the lab and are now scaling it for real-world deployment. Our mission is to commercialize breakthrough energy technology that makes clean, heavy-duty power practical and affordable.
